Singer hometowns per genre

Our project studies the geographic locations of the hometowns of the artists in the top 100 songs each year separated by genre. By selecting one or more of the genres below, you can see the number of artists from each state that made it to the top 100 songs in the year indicated by the slider. The top 5 states are indicated on the right and you can hover your mouse over any of the states in the map to see the exact number of artists from that state.
